Chapter 370  
Main Character of Life (2)  
~*~

Talent had only ever been an excuse.

From the very beginning, their minds had been too full of magic for success or fame to take root there.

They wanted to win. They did not want to lose in competition. And in the end, they wanted to become the greatest wizards.

Dante’s eyes gleamed when he saw Inferno. It was powerful magic, the kind of spell one would never expect to see in a graduation exam.

‘She lowered the entropy using self-image memory. If it were Red Eyes, it would be close to zero percent. This will work.’

“Dante. Dante.”

Lyria, who was on her knees, called to Dante behind her in a trembling voice.

“What is it? Is something wrong?”

“Can we really… do this? Can we succeed?”

“Why are you asking me that?”

Lyria turned her head and looked up at him. Her face was wet with tears, and it was clear she was caught in a fierce inner struggle.

“You always make sure of your choices! So tell me! If you say it, I think I’ll be able to feel at ease.”

Dante understood how she felt. A mage’s composure in battle was as important as a swordsman’s conviction. Fear did not come from danger itself, but from uncertainty. When someone could analyze the situation objectively, their comrades found the courage to fight.

“I’m not optimistic,” Dante said. “But I’m not pessimistic either.”

It was, quite literally, two sides of the same coin. And for a wizard, this was the minimum probability required to attempt something.

“There’s a fifty percent chance Shirone can push it back. There’s a fifty percent chance my defensive magic circle will hold. And there’s a fifty percent chance Amy’s magic will affect the enemy. If you add those three cases together, do you understand what situation we’re in?”

Lyria silently repeated Dante’s words, then raised her head with wide eyes as though she had realized something.

“So… one hundred and fifty percent?”

Dante gave a bitter smile.

“…You really aren’t a wizard.”

Remaining distance to Park Yo-jin’s activation point: 220 meters.

Shirone, in a trance, refined the Photon Cannon. The flashes of light overflowing from the backs of his hands were blinding, and the magic amplification spheres embedded in both hands glowed red-hot.

“Quaaaaaaaaaaaa!”

The back of Thousand Arms Guanyin bent as it was driven toward the ground.

Remaining distance: 200 meters.

Zocre’s legs trembled as he stared at the gigantic Guanyin, which looked as if it might collapse at any moment.

‘That’s Shirone…’

A person who could overwhelm Dante, the kingdom’s greatest defensive force, with sheer firepower.

Even so, there had been times when Zocre had thought of Shirone as still being at a student’s level. Otherwise, he could not have endured the shame.

But the Photon Cannon he was seeing with his own eyes held power that even most professionals could not use.

They might not surpass professionals in every aspect, but in at least one area, they had already reached the professional level.

That was what it meant to be one of the top five prestigious students.

Remaining distance: 170 meters.

“It’s coming in! I’ll create the second barrier!”

Dante trapped Thousand Arms Guanyin inside the Hexagonal Mansion. Because the information had been accumulated only on the inside of the magic circle, attacks from the outside were not applied. On the other hand, Thousand Arms Guanyin, trapped within the narrow walls, thrashed violently.

“Ghh!”

Dante’s face twisted. He was pulling the Mansion down toward the ground with Thousand Arms Guanyin locked inside it, but the resistance was stronger than he had expected. In particular, the fireballs scattering in every direction carried enough power to destroy even a defensive magic circle built for maximum durability within thirty seconds.

Remaining distance: 120 meters.

Dante shouted, veins bulging across his face.

“Amy! Now!”

“Got it!”

Amy leaped up and swung her hands. The massive Inferno left its owner and drifted away. The ball of fire slowly crossed the sky, following the coordinates of the self-image memory, and struck Thousand Arms Guanyin without the slightest error.

“Quaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaa!”

As Thousand Arms Guanyin’s body burned, Dante felt the pressure ease slightly. Flame-type magic had an advantage over the Photon Cannon in that it could inflict continuous impact.

Remaining distance: 40 meters.

Lyria shook her head and shouted.

“It’s still not enough! At this rate, we can’t confine it in Park Yo-jin!”

The burning Thousand Arms Guanyin screamed as it clawed at the magic circle inside the Mansion. At last, the magic circle, pushed past its durability limit, shattered like glass.

Lyria’s face went pale.

“It’s over. The demigod will be released into the world.”

“No, it’s not over.”

Lyria looked up at the sky.

Shirone was high above them, gathering a brilliant light in his hands as he looked down at Thousand Arms Guanyin. As the light in his hands swelled, it was as if another sun had appeared in the sky.

“Yaaaaaa!”

Shirone fired the Photon Cannon with all his might. A massive beam of light, one meter in diameter, pierced Thousand Arms Guanyin’s side.

Remaining distance: 30 meters.

“Quaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaa!”

Thousand Arms Guanyin flailed with its many arms and struggled. However, the mass of light, too great even for its own laws to withstand, continued to assault it.

Remaining distance: 20 meters.

“Now! Begin!”

Lyria clasped her hands together and prayed.

May evil be destroyed. May there be a world where everyone can live happily. If only she could achieve a paradise of goodness with no hunger, no murder, and no adultery, she would not care if her body were torn into a thousand or ten thousand pieces. Even if the devil hated her and she fell into hell to suffer for eternity, she would be able to laugh. She would become fertilizer for the world. If everyone could possess a good will, she would gladly go into the filthiest place and roll her body through it.

Tears poured down her face like a waterfall.

Her will, transcending the limits of an ordinary person, drew in the energy of the totem embedded in the old castle. As the Geum Ma-jin disappeared, Park Yo-jin, ten meters in diameter, was revealed.

“I command in the name of Lyria! Evil, sleep here!”

Lyria’s eyes burned gold. Her streaming tears evaporated in an instant, and a pillar of light formed from law pierced the sky.

“Kuaaaaa!”

As the flash of the Photon Cannon pressed forward, Thousand Arms Guanyin screamed in agony and struggled with dozens of hands.

Remaining distance: 10 meters.

“Aaaaaaa!”

Shirone felt the output of the Photon Cannon begin to fall. But when he raised his power again through mental transcendence, the beam amplified and completely engulfed Thousand Arms Guanyin.

Remaining distance: 0 meters.

The moment Thousand Arms Guanyin entered Park Yo-jin’s domain, Lyria concentrated all of her will into one point.

“Kyaaaaaaaaaaaaa!”

The most powerful golden magic of the Akeanis Shrine.

Jade King Park Yo-jin.

Two arms made of light rose up, twisting together like braided rope, seized Thousand Arms Guanyin, and dragged it toward the ground.

“Ah! Ghh! Aaaaaa!”

Thousand Arms Guanyin endured desperately. There was no reason for evil laws to exist in a space filled with good will. Even so, the fact that it could resist was clear proof that it was a demigod.

‘But it’s over! Just a little… just a little more…!’

Just as Lyria thought it was finished, she heard footsteps. Vivian passed by her and began running toward Jade King Park Yo-jin.

“Ice Queen!”

Dante’s face contorted.

“That fool!”

Lyria shouted in shock.

“You can’t get close! You’ll be dragged in!”

Park Yo-jin was different from the Geum Ma-jin. Because good will was concentrated into such a narrow area, any human even slightly inclined toward evil would inevitably fall into the abyss.

Vivian clearly heard Lyria’s voice.

But she did not stop running.

“This is the end. This is where my play ends.”

It had been a life no one had paid attention to. But the Ice Queen had given her hope that she could become the main character. Even if that being was a monster, even if its kindness and consideration had been born from ignorance, that had been Vivian’s only spotlight.

Tears streamed from Vivian’s eyes.

What would happen if she entered Park Yo-jin? Perhaps only the stillness that followed the end of a play would remain forever.

But she was not afraid. She would meet the Ice Queen. She would take her hand and greet the audience. She would thank the world for watching such a magnificent performance.

“Yaaaaaa!”

Blinded by tears, Vivian kicked off the ground and threw herself toward Jade King Park Yo-jin.

“Vivian! Come back!”

Zocre shouted, sobbing.

She was a sensitive child. Perhaps she did not even realize that her heart had broken in the face of a situation she could not bear.

But she could not end like this.

It was all his fault.

If only he had not, drunk on himself, delved into the Ice Queen’s nature. If only he had not cared about Amy. Then Vivian would never have made such an extreme choice.

“Vivian!”

Zocre’s scream could not catch Vivian.

Amy did.

Amy threw herself forward and wrapped her arms around Vivian. They escaped the boundary by a hair’s breadth and fell to the ground. At the same time, Thousand Arms Guanyin sank as though being buried inside Jade King Park Yo-jin. The sound of hundreds of claws scraping across the ground was horrifying.

“Kuaaaaa!”

Everyone watching shuddered as the ground was plowed apart by that force. However, even such formidable power could not overcome the reason of Jade King Park Yo-jin.

It was the power of a mysterious and profound law.

Thousand Arms Guanyin sank into the abyss of that law, leaving only thousands of fingernail marks behind.

Vivian, who had been watching through her tears, turned to Amy and shouted.

“Let go! I said let go! I’m going in too!”

“Are you crazy? If you go in there, you’ll die!”

“Since when did you care about me? Do you know how I feel? You’ve lived your whole life like the main character. How could you know how miserable I am?”

“So you want to die? Because you’re miserable?”

“No, I’m going to torment you until the very end! I’ll never let you succeed! At this moment, I’m the main character!”

“Don’t talk nonsense, idiot! What kind of main character gives up on life so easily?”

Amy’s voice pierced Vivian’s ears.

“It’s hard for everyone to fight! But you fight until the end to become the main character! If you give up—if you give up…!”

Amy choked on her own words and lowered her head.

She, too, was someone who had wanted to be the main character more than anyone.

“From that moment on, you become nothing.”

The feeling of being cast out of the world’s spotlight.

While everyone looked only at the successful candidates, Amy had cried alone in her room.

She had not let anyone see her.

But now, she felt as though she finally understood.

You still had to fight.

Even if a bad ending waited at the end, those who did not fight to the very end would never be given the lead role.

Vivian stared blankly, unable to understand Amy’s sudden outburst. Then she opened her mouth as if to say something.

“I…”

Then the ground began to tremble.

Vivian turned her head, and everyone else looked toward Park Yo-jin as well.

The moment Shirone landed on the ground, he shouted.

“It’s not over yet!”

Dozens of hands rose from Jade King Park Yo-jin. As the upright arms bent at the same time and pushed against the ground, the face of Thousand Arms Guanyin emerged once more.

“I! I am the ruler of the world!”

The faces of Shirone and the others went pale. It had still not completely overcome the power of Jade King Park Yo-jin, but no one knew what would happen if it were given time.

“You managed to catch the lawman? You were lucky.”

Shirone turned his head in surprise.

A woman stood there with curly blond hair, dark skin, and a suit that clung tightly to her body.

It was Ahoya Kang Nan, chief secretary of the Magic Association.

“Who are you?”

Kang Nan took off her high heels as she walked toward Guanyin. Passing Shirone’s group, she said,

“Stay back. You might get hurt.”

Lyria shouted.

“Wait! You can’t just go in there! If you can’t handle the law, you’ll be dragged into Park Yo-jin’s power!”

Kang Nan did not show the slightest hesitation as she stepped into Jade King Park Yo-jin. She did not know whether she was a person inclined toward good or evil. However, she had lived as a Ram Muai from the moment she was born, and her convictions were not so weak that they could be shaken by the law.

“Aaaaaa! Aaaaaaa!”

Thousand Arms Guanyin repeatedly rose and sank, clawing at the ground again and again like a person drowning in the sea.
